Enrollment as a Ph.D. student in Denmark
Created 2020-06-27| Updated 2020-06-27|LifeThoughts
Word count:376|Reading time: 1 min
| Post View:|Comments:

哇！我真的太羡慕了，丹麦的博士生待遇也太好了。

PhD, Postdoc, and Professor Salaries in Denmark

From: https://academicpositions.com/career-advice/phd-postdoc-and-professor-salaries-in-denmark

The average monthly salary for a PhD fellow at the University of Copenhagen is 32,567 DKK. 2017年的时候，博士生的平均月收入就到了32567丹麦克朗，

The average monthly salary for a postdoc at the University of Copenhagen is 40,849 DKK.

The average monthly salary for an assistant professor at the University of Copenhagen is 42,961 DKK.

The average monthly salary for a professor at the University of Copenhagen is 71,798 DKK.

不过哥本哈根大学的教授的工资好像不够多呀。

中国的工资水平是强烈两极分化的，我们院的老师基本工资起码也要40-50万人民币/年，但是学生只有600*10+1000*10=16000/年，老师们的工资至少是学生的30倍左右。而且我们知道，老师们是可以从经费里拿一部分钱给自己的，所以综合下来大概在复旦，这个比率可以达到40倍。

Employment as a PhD student at the University of Copenhagen

As a PhD student you will be provided with a computer, office phone and an office, as well as general office supplies.

妈耶，我真的酸了，太羡慕了。

哥本哈根的博士会给电脑，办公室，电话还有常用的办公室用品。复旦的屁都没有。电脑还是自己买的，当然我知道有年轻老师给学生买了电脑，还是Macbook Pro，但是他买完估计也后悔了。

唉，羡慕啊。
